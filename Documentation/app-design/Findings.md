# Findings - App Design

## 2026-05-02 - Competitive Research Insights

- Market growing at 24.6% CAGR, projected $11.57B by 2034.
- All major retailers building walled-garden AI assistants (Instacart, Kroger, Walmart, Amazon, Albertsons).
- No app combines: AI recipe parsing + multi-store matching + price optimization + specialty ingredient sourcing.
- International/specialty cuisine ingredient sourcing is a massive unserved gap.
- Instacart Developer Platform provides public API with 85K+ retailers and 5% affiliate commission.
- Kroger is the only major US chain with a fully public developer API.
- Best ingredient parsing: hybrid approach (deterministic NLP + LLM structured output) achieves 95.9% F1.
- Users delete meal planning apps because they require too much input — low friction is critical.
- Yummly ($100M acquisition) shut down in 2024 — proof that non-AI recipe apps are dying.
